Lake life / notes

Boat. Family.
Hill Country.

Canyon Lake is one of the cleanest, deepest reservoirs in Texas: green water, limestone shorelines, and the kind of dramatic Hill Country sky that earns the photos. We have been on it long enough that it stopped being a destination and started being the way our weeks reset.

The boat is where my son wants the wheel, where the best conversations land, and where the noise of the workweek finally backs off. Some of the most important talks I have had with him have happened with the engine idling and the shoreline a little farther away than usual.

This page works better when it feels intentional, so the scattered image wall is gone. What is left is the tighter set: the crew, the driver's seat, and the water itself.
